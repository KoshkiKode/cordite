## Cohesion Pipeline

Some models (quaternius/, kenney/, majadroid/) have been processed through our Blender
cohesion pipeline (`assets/pipeline/`) which applies:
- Flat-shading normalization
- Vertex color baking from texture atlases
- Scale/rotation standardization
- Consistent naming conventions

This processing is explicitly permitted under CC0, which grants unrestricted rights to
"reproduce, adapt, distribute, perform, display, communicate, and translate a Work"
for "any purpose whatsoever, including without limitation commercial purposes."
